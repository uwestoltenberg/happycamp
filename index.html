Why Resilience Matters

My name is Uwe, and my life has been shaped by resilience. I survived a near-fatal accident that left me in an artificial coma for several days and overcame multiple burnouts that tested me deeply.

I spent 15 years running my own software company and 20 years working for the United Nations. During this time, I worked with people from many cultures around the world, learning from their perspectives and gaining the energy to help others.

Now, I am using everything I’ve learned to create the Happy-Camp that will help people rebuild their resilience, achieve their goals, and make lasting changes in their lives.

The Happy-Camp Philosophy

I believe resilience is something everyone is born with. Our ancestors relied on strong resilience to survive in a challenging world, and this strength still exists within all of us.

However, modern life has pulled us away from what keeps us resilient. We face stress without the natural outlets of fighting or escaping. Our diets are filled with processed foods and excess carbs, and we don’t move enough because life has become too convenient.

This modern lifestyle is breaking down our natural resilience—but we have the power to change it!
