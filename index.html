Meet Uwe – The Mind Behind Happy Camp

My name is Uwe, and I’ve spent decades transforming my life through resilience and health. From overcoming personal challenges like weight loss and burnout to designing a fitness camp for others, I’ve made it my mission to help people unlock their potential and embrace lasting change.

The Happy-Camp Philosophy

We believe resilience is something everyone is born with. Our ancestors relied on strong resilience to survive in a challenging world, and this strength still exists within all of us.

However, modern life has pulled us away from what keeps us resilient. We face stress without the natural outlets of fighting or escaping. Our diets are filled with processed foods and excess carbs, and we don’t move enough because life has become too convenient.

This modern lifestyle is breaking down our natural resilience—but we have the power to change it!
