Project Road to 2027

1. Conceptualization of Happy-Camp

2024

Refining the vision, setting objectives, and planning the foundation of the project.

2024 is where it all begins. This phase is about transforming an idea into a vision with purpose. It’s the time to define what Happy-Camp stands for—its mission, its values, and its promise to help people rediscover resilience.
This is the foundation for everything to come. Imagine the bungalows, the fitness areas, the community, and the moments of growth and transformation participants will experience. It’s a journey of creativity, planning, and shaping a future where Happy-Camp becomes a reality.

2. Securing the Land and Building Awareness

2025

Purchasing the required property and initiating marketing efforts (including this website) to build interest and a community around Happy-Camp.

2025 is the year—where the vision of Happy-Camp begins to take physical shape. Finding the right land is about more than just a location; it’s about discovering a place where transformation can happen, a space that inspires growth and resilience. The legal steps are carefully managed to ensure that everything is ready for a sustainable and meaningful journey ahead.
This phase is also about connection. It’s about reaching out to people who share the belief that resilience can change lives. Marketing efforts, including this website, are launched to share the story of Happy-Camp and invite others to join the mission. Building a strong community of like-minded individuals is key, as is finding partners—local suppliers, wellness experts, and collaborators—who align with the goal of creating something extraordinary.
This is the year where Happy-Camp moves from a dream to a shared reality, with a space to grow and a network of support to help it thrive.

3: Laying the Groundwork

October 2025

Preparing the site, obtaining necessary permits, and establishing essential infrastructure to support future construction.

October 2025 marks the beginning of tangible progress. This is where the future home of Happy-Camp begins to take shape. The land, full of potential, is prepared for its transformation. Roads are mapped, utilities are planned, and the groundwork is carefully laid to create a space where resilience and renewal can thrive.
This phase is about building the foundation—literally and metaphorically—for everything to come. From leveling the ground to preparing spaces for the bungalows, common areas, and activity zones, each step is a reminder that great things start with a solid base.
It’s also a time of patience and precision. Every permit, every infrastructure decision, and every piece of the puzzle is carefully aligned with the vision of creating a harmonious and sustainable Happy-Camp.

4: Bringing the Vision to Life

June 2026

Constructing key facilities, including participant bungalows, the common kitchen, calisthenics park, and other activity areas.

June 2026 is when Happy-Camp truly begins to take shape. This phase is all about turning carefully laid plans into reality. The bungalows are constructed, offering cozy and welcoming spaces for participants to rest and recharge. The common kitchen and dining area come to life, ready to host shared meals and moments of connection.
Outside, the calisthenics park and fitness areas begin to take form—spaces designed to inspire movement and strength. The meditation and yoga zones are created to be sanctuaries of peace and mindfulness. Every brick, every beam, and every detail reflects the commitment to building a place where resilience can flourish.

5: Putting on the Finishing Touches

June 2027

Finalizing the facilities, testing programs, and onboarding the test participants to ensure a smooth launch.

June 2027 is a time of preparation and excitement. With the construction complete, the focus shifts to refining every detail to ensure everything is just right. Facilities are inspected, adjusted, and polished to create a seamless experience for participants.
This phase is also about building the right team. Recruiting and training staff who share the vision of Happy-Camp is a top priority. From keto experts to fitness trainers, yoga instructors, and surf coaches, each person is carefully selected to contribute to a supportive and inspiring environment.
Programs are trialed and perfected, ensuring that meal plans, workout routines, and mindfulness practices meet the highest standards. Every detail is aligned to make participants feel welcome and empowered from the moment they arrive.

6: Grand Opening of Happy-Camp 🥂 💃

August 2027

Welcoming the first participants and officially launching Happy-Camp’s unique, holistic experience.

August 2027 marks the official launch of Happy-Camp—a moment of pride, excitement, and new beginnings. The first participants arrive, stepping into a space designed to transform lives through resilience, community, and holistic well-being.
The opening is celebrated with a warm and welcoming event, bringing together participants, staff, and partners to share the vision of Happy-Camp. It’s a time to reflect on the journey so far and to set the stage for the many stories of growth and transformation yet to come.
With the programs ready, the facilities complete, and the team energized, Happy-Camp opens its doors to begin its mission of inspiring strength, balance, and lasting change.
